Lecturer, Q1 and Research Awards

November 25, 2022

2022

Today I received several great news. First, I have been awarded PUCPR’s Excellence in Scientific Research - Q1 award. On top of that, I received the news that among my peers, I have published the paper at the journal with the highest impact factor. This is the third time I received this award in the last four years.

I am also delighted that I have also been awarded as Awarded as one of the best-ranked lecturers at PUCPR (Prêmio de Excelência no Ensino) and also one of the best-ranked researchers (Prêmio de Excelência em Pesquisa).
